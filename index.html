About US

We are a community dedicated towards creating the world as it stands today in minecraft, 1:1 scale. This website is specifically dedicated to the Illinois community and it's Minecraft server and Discord.

We have our own Minecraft server which is currently under development, scroll down to see the server status.

FAQ

How do I join the community?
You can join both the Illinois and Global Discords under the tab "Join Us."

How do I become a builder?
To become a builder you must fill out the builder application in the Global Discord under the channel #applications.

How do I get whitelisted on the server?
To get whitelisted you must join both Discord servers, become an official BTE builder, and fill out the whitelist form.
